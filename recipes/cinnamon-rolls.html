Description

A cinnamon roll is a sweet roll commonly served in Northern Europe and North America.

A cinnamon roll consists of a rolled sheet of yeast-leavened dough onto which a cinnamon and sugar mixture is sprinkled over a thin coat of butter. The dough is then rolled, cut into individual portions and baked.

Ingredients

  • 1 (1 pound) loaf frozen bread dough, thawed
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 2/3 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon water, or as needed
  • 1/3 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 2/3 cup sifted confectioners' sugar
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1 dash vanilla extract
